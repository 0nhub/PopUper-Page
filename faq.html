Popuper is the best solution to convert links to popup windows.

Add your link to the field and press enter, or select one of the most visited internet services and focus on your tasks.

We make it easier to focus on your thoughts. Use the minimalistic browser window interface.

Popuper is free, we don't publish any ads and we don't track your data either. If you want to support us, please tell your friends about this project.

With the browser extension, you can convert pages directly to pop-ups by pressing the extension icon.
